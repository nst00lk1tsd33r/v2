Chrome Extension Installation Guide

1. Unpack the Asset

Locate the downloaded .zip file on your machine and extract its contents into a dedicated folder.

2. Enable Developer Mode

Navigate to chrome://extensions in your browser window, and toggle the Developer Mode switch in the top-right corner active.

3. Load Unpacked Extension

Click on Load unpacked and select your extracted folder, or simply drag and drop the folder anywhere into the browser window viewport.
